The GirlScript Summer of Code (GSSoC) is a 3-month open-source program held annually from March to May, aimed at introducing beginners to open-source development. Open to participants worldwide, it provides mentorship, diverse project opportunities, and rewards like certificates, internships, and prizes for top contributors. GSSoC fosters skill-building, networking, and career growth, making it an inclusive platform for learning and collaboration. Registration typically begins in February.

Eligibility:

Open to all genders and skill levels globally.

Features:

  • Beginner-friendly with mentorship support.
  • Diverse project options to match interests.
  • Rewards include cash prizes, internships, certificates, and merchandise.

Benefits:

Skill enhancement, networking, career opportunities.

Registration:

Opens in February/March
