The Adobe Women in Tech Scholarship is designed to support and empower women pursuing careers in technology by providing financial assistance and mentorship opportunities. This scholarship is open to female students currently enrolled in a bachelor’s or master’s degree program in computer science, computer engineering, or related technical fields. In addition to the monetary award, recipients gain access to a network of professionals at Adobe and are offered career development resources, internships, and the chance to be a part of Adobe’s inclusive and innovative community. The scholarship aims to inspire and encourage more women to pursue and succeed in tech careers.

Eligibility:

  • Be an Indian female citizen student
  • Must be enrolled as a full-time student in a formal technology 4-year BE / B.Tech education program or an Integrated ME/MS/MTech program at an Indian University or Institute at the time of applying and should be completing the program in the Academic year 2024
  • Should be pursuing a Major or Minor in the following streams: Computer Science/Engineering, Information Science, Data Science, Electrical/Electronics Engineering, Math, and Computing
  • May apply to ONLY ONE of the following two Scholarships – Adobe India Women-In-Technology Scholarship OR the Adobe Global Women In Technology Scholarship
  • Anyone who is a “related party” to an Adobe employee is not eligible to apply
  • Definition of Related Party – Any person having the following relationship with an Adobe employee: spouse, domestic partner, child, parent, sibling, child-in-law, parent-in-law, or sibling-in-law; any other relative or person who lives in the household of an Adobe employee

Features:

  • Internship interview opportunity at Adobe India
  • Covers remaining university education until the academic year
  • Mentorship from senior technology leaders at Adobe.
  • All-expenses-paid trip to the Grace Hopper Conference, India

Benefits:

  • Internship opportunity at a leading tech company like Adobe.
  • Eases the burden of tuition fees for the recipient’s remaining education.
  • Access to personalized mentorship from top industry professionals.
  • Connect with global leaders in tech at the Grace Hopper Conference.
  • Enhanced visibility and career prospects in the tech industry.

Registration:

Application Opens usually in November or December.
