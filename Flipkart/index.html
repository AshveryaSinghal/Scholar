The Flipkart Girl Wanna Code program is an initiative aimed at encouraging and empowering young women to pursue careers in technology and coding. The program provides a platform for female students to enhance their coding skills, participate in hands-on projects, and receive mentorship from Flipkart professionals. It focuses on providing opportunities for women to enter the tech industry, develop leadership skills, and build a strong technical foundation

Eligibility:

  • All women engineering students pursuing a full-time B.Tech./ B.E. ( 3rd and 4th year) or M.Tech./ M.E. (All Years) degrees across branches from selected institutes.

Features:

  • Intensive training sessions to enhance programming and technical skills.
  • Guidance from industry professionals at Flipkart.
  • Real-world coding projects to build practical experience.
  • Connect with like-minded women and tech professionals.
  • Potential for internship or full-time roles at Flipkart.

Benefits:

  • Learn from experts and improve coding skills through a structured program.
  • Build a strong portfolio and gain exposure to job opportunities at Flipkart.
  • Develop leadership and problem-solving skills through mentorship and projects.
  • Join a community of women in tech and expand professional connections.
  • Gain the confidence to pursue a tech career and take on leadership roles.

Registration:

Application Opens usually in January or February.
